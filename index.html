Who Am I?

A brief look into my world, mindset, and journey in cybersecurity.

I'm Jeewaka Jayashan, a cybersecurity-focused student at SLIIT (Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology) pursuing a passion for keeping digital infrastructure safe.
